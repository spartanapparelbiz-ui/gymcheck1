/ COMMON QUESTIONS

QUICK questions.

STILL UNSURE?READ THESE
How accurate is it really?
It catches the big things a coach would catch in a quick look — knees caving, back rounding, depth, bar path, hip movement. It's not a substitute for an elite strength coach who knows your specific history. But for the 95% of lifters who train alone and have never had a coach watch them, it's a massive upgrade over guessing.
What lifts can I check?
Squat (back & front), bench press, deadlift (and RDL), overhead press, barbell row, hip thrust, pull-up, dip — and a generic "Other" option for anything else. Just film from the side for the best read.
Do you store my videos?
No. Videos are processed in real time and never saved. Free users have nothing stored at all. Pro users get the result (score, verdict, flags) saved so they can track progress — but the original clip is never kept.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. One click in the billing portal. No call to cancel, no "are you sure" loops, no email forms. You'll keep your remaining checks until the end of your billing month, then you're done.
I'm a beginner — is this for me?
Especially for you. Beginners have the most to gain because bad habits cement fast. Getting honest feedback on day 30 vs day 300 is the difference between "I lift 3x/wk" and "I'm rehabbing a herniated disc."
Will this replace my coach?
If your coach is a programming/nutrition/mindset guy: no, those things matter and AI can't do them. If your coach is purely a "watch me squat" guy that you pay $80/hr for: yes, this can probably replace 80% of what you're getting — for a tiny fraction of the cost.
